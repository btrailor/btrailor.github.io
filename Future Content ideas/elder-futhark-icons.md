Commercial digital icon set bringing ancient runic forms into modern UI design.

## Concept

Hand-designed vector icons for each of the 24 Elder Futhark runes. Filling a market gap for clean, modern rune iconography suitable for games, apps, and digital products.

## Style Variants

- **Filled** — Solid forms for high contrast
- **Outlined** — Stroke-based for lighter interfaces
- **Rounded** — Soft corners for friendly aesthetics
- **Sharp** — Angular precision for bold statements

## Philosophy

All icons manually designed in vector software. No AI generation—preserving the intentionality and craft that connects to the runic tradition.
