Browser-based euclidean rhythm generator with visual feedback. Implements the Bjorklund algorithm for creating polyrhythmic patterns commonly found in traditional music from around the world.

**Features:**
- Up to 8 simultaneous tracks
- Real-time parameter adjustment
- Pattern save/load
- MIDI output support
- Responsive touch interface
