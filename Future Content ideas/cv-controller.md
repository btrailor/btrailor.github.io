Custom control voltage generator for eurorack modular synthesizers. Features 4 independent CV outputs, MIDI-to-CV conversion, and programmable LFO/envelope generators.

**Specifications:**
- 4x 12-bit CV outputs (0-10V)
- USB MIDI interface
- 16 preset slots
- Open-source hardware design
- Compact 8HP eurorack module
