## About

Brett Gershon is a sound artist, instrument builder, and developer based in Ypsilanti, Michigan. Working at the intersection of code, electronics, and sound, Brett creates tools and compositions that explore the musical potential of errors, accidents, and emergent systems.

## Current Projects

- Building modular synthesizer modules
- Developing browser-based music tools in RNBO
- Performing live with custom software/hardware
- Writing about music technology and creative process
- Building "Optimize.Me", a satirical mobile game . .

**Code:** JavaScript, Python, C++, Lua
**Web:** React, Hugo, Node.js, Jekyll, Bootstrap, Sass,
